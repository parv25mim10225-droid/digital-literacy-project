SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS

DO'S:
1. Maintain a professional profile  
2. Share useful and positive content  
3. Protect your personal information  
4. Verify information before sharing  
5. Be respectful in comments  

DON'TS:
1. Don’t post inappropriate content  
2. Don’t share fake news  
3. Don’t use offensive language  
4. Don’t overshare personal details  
5. Don’t engage in online arguments
