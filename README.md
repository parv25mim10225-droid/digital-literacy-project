Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course.
The aim of this project is to understand and apply digital skills such as online safety, professional communication, 
and use of digital platforms. Through this project, I performed five different tasks that helped me build awareness 
and practical knowledge of the digital world.

Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva.
The infographic explains key concepts such as digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices,
and email etiquette. This task helped me understand how to present information visually in a clear and engaging way.

Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
In this task, I created my digital portfolio by setting up profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle.
GitHub is used for showcasing coding projects, LinkedIn helps in professional networking,
and Kaggle is useful for learning and practicing data-related skills.
This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional online presence.

Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this task, I explored coding and collaboration tools. I solved a beginner-level problem on HackerRank,
which improved my logical thinking and coding skills. I also created a Google Form to test digital literacy awareness 
and linked it with a Google Sheet to collect responses.

 Google Form Link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc8LRisxIM9DbNJOS9oGoUYSRP-WWouW992KXpZEERTYv3TjA/viewform?usp=publish-editor

Task 4 – Email Etiquette
In this task, I wrote two professional emails: one for requesting an assignment extension 
and another for applying for an internship. I also created a checklist of social media Do’s and Don’ts. 
This task helped me understand the importance of clear and professional communication.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I studied UPI fraud as a cybercrime case study. I understood how such frauds happen and who is usually targeted. 
I also created a prevention checklist to help users stay safe online. This task increased my awareness about digital security.
