STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
2. Do not accept unknown payment requests  
3. Always verify the sender before making transactions  
4. Use strong and unique passwords  
5. Enable two-factor authentication on accounts  
6. Avoid clicking on suspicious links  
7. Regularly update your apps and software  
8. Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi  
9. Check app authenticity before downloading  
10. Monitor your bank transactions regularly  

UPI SAFETY TIPS:
- UPI PIN is only for sending money, not receiving  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes
